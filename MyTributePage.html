About Albert Einstein

☛ Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879 in Württemberg Germany.

☛ At age 16 he renounced his German citizenship and became a citizen of Switzerland.

☛ He married Mileva Maric who was the only female student in his university physics class

☛ He was a civil rights activist and a supporter of free speech.

☛Despite having such a fantastic intellect he struggled in language classes.

☛ He dropped out of school at age 15.

☛ He wrote his first scholarly paper at age 16.

☛ He created the E=mc2 formula.

☛ He won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921.

☛ Despite rumors he did not his math classes as a child.

☛ He offered to give his wife his Nobel Prize during their divorce settlement.

☛ He has a daughter who mysteriously went misssing and was never found.

☛ He was spied on by the FBI for decades.

☛ He urged the construction of the nuclear bomb, and later supported nuclear disarmament.

☛ He was asked to become the president of Israel.

☛ following his death his brain was preserved after an autopsy in hopes of uncovering the reason for his genius intellect.

☛ His theory of relativity made him an overnight celebrity.
